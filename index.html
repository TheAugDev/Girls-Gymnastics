Our Story

Meet Sarah & Elizabeth

We are a proud Filipino-American family, and our daughters, Sarah (Inday) and Elizabeth (Chichay), are dedicated competitive gymnasts representing Fairland Gymnastics. They pour their hearts and souls into their sport, training countless hours each week.

Their coaches see something truly special in them. We were recently given the incredible news that our youngest, Elizabeth (Chichay), is being groomed by an Olympic coach with the potential to one day follow in the footsteps of trampoline champions!

This journey requires immense dedication, not just from them, but from our whole family. Competition fees, travel, coaching, and equipment costs add up quickly. We want to give them every opportunity to chase their dreams.
